üß† What is Clipboard Hijacking?

Clipboard hijacking is a cyber attack technique where malicious JavaScript code intercepts your copy operations and replaces the intended content with something else entirely. This happens invisibly - you see one thing, but something completely different gets copied to your clipboard.

This technique is commonly exploited in cryptocurrency scams, phishing attacks, and malware distribution. Attackers often replace legitimate wallet addresses, URLs, or commands with malicious alternatives.
